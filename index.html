Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

Musica respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data, permissions, and advertisements.

By using Musica, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection

Musica does not collect, store, or share any personal user information.

The app works completely offline except for third-party advertisements served through Google AdMob.

Permissions Used

Musica requires access to audio and media files stored on your device for:

  • Listing music files available on the device
  • Playing music selected by the user
  • Providing equalizer and audio enhancement features

Your music files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Advertisements

Musica uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

This data is handled directly by Google according to their privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

Musica does not sell, trade, or share user personal data with third parties.

Internet Usage

The app does not use internet services except for loading advertisements through Google AdMob.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

All music playback and media operations occur locally on the user's device.

No user data is stored on external servers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any future changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
